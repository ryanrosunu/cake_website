About Us

Who are we?

We are a local home style bakery committed to making quality cakes and desserts! Our owner Emily started her journey baking at home when she was just a teenager and now she opened her own designated location to serve everyone in and out of the neighborhood!

Where are we?

We are located in Evanston, Illinois just off of Foster Street. You are welcome to stop by anytime to check out our in stock cakes and designs!
